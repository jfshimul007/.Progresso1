About .Progresso

.Progresso is a robust, user-friendly academic management system that empowers educational institutions to track and manage attendance and academic progress with ease.

Our platform offers teachers, administrators, and students a seamless way to interact, monitor performance, and make data-driven decisions that promote academic success. Whether you're managing a single class or an entire institution, .Progresso helps you stay organized and focused on student success.

Why Choose .Progresso?

Free Attendance Tracking

Progresso is a powerful yet simple online tool for tracking attendance, helping teachers and instructors manage classes efficiently.

Student Progress Reports

Track students’ academic progress with real-time reporting and insights, helping educators make informed decisions.

Parent Communication

Easily send notifications and updates to parents about student attendance, grades, and performance.
